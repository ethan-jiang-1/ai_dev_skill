## 关键事实

- The skill presents itself as a way to draft or revise human-sounding prose.
- It explicitly targets the problem of avoiding stereotypical LLM writing style.
- It encodes writing craft rules such as:
  - make the point early
  - active voice
  - concrete verbs
  - remove filler
